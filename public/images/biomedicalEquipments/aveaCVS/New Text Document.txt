AVEA™ CVS Ventilation System

For clinicians and administrators in the acute care environment, the Avea™ CVS is a comprehensive neonatal through adult 
ventilation system with the complete package of all advanced features and maneuvers in one device. It helps empower 
clinicians at the bedside to improve patient outcomes and decrease costs-of-care, while the Respiratory Knowledge 
Portal receives and processes data, converting it to actionable insights to help clinicians improve respiratory care processes.
