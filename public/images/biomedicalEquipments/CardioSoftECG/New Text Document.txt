CardioSoft ECG

CardioSoft is a CAM-USB hardware, PC-based 12-Lead ECG system that is the perfect extension of your metabolic cart, providing 
you with clinical excellence for complete cardiopulmonary exercise testing (CPET).
The CardioSoft ECG system is a comprehensive system for precise acquisition, evaluation and reporting of your ECG data, 
but it is also surprisingly easy to use. Everything is on the main testing screen and quickly accessible with a simple mouse click.
